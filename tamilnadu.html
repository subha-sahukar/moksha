Visiting Guidelines

  • Attire: Traditional or modest clothing is recommended (dhoti/sari for main sanctum access in many temples).
  • Timings: Most temples open early (06:00–12:00) and again in the evening (16:00–20:00); check local pages for festival schedules.
  • Etiquette: Remove shoes, avoid loud photography in puja areas, and respect priestly timings for seva.
  • Auspicious days: Vaikunta Ekadasi, Pradosham, Skanda Sashti, and local Brahmotsavams are peak pilgrimage times.
