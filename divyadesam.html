The Twelve Alwars

Poigai Alwar First among Alwars — mystical devotion in rain-water pond.
Bhuthath Alwar Deep yogic insight, celebrated for inner light.
Pey Alwar Saw Perumal & Thayar together — ecstatic divine vision.
Thirumazhisai Alwar Worship of Naranan — emphasized true surrender.
Nammalwar “Bhagavad Vishayam” poet — heart melts for Perumal.
Madhurakavi Alwar Devotion to Nammalwar — guru bhakti ideal.
Kulashekara Alwar King-saint — yearning to serve at Lord’s feet.
Periyalwar Witnessed baby Krishna — composed protective praise.
Andal Divine heroine, Margazhi devotion queen.
Thondaradipodi Alwar Simple flower-servant — pure bhakti path.
Thiruppan Alwar Sang of Ranganatha’s beauty limb-by-limb.
Thirumangai Alwar Warrior-saint — protector of Divya Desams.

Divya Desam Yatra Guide

  • Start before sunrise — Suprabhata Seva
  • Chant Govinda Nama while walking prakaram
  • Offer tulasi & milk prasad
  • Avoid onion/garlic on temple days
  • Read at least one Alwar pasuram daily (English meaning fine)
