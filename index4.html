Meenakshi Amman Temple

Thiruparankundram

Murugan temple — hill shrine, skanda legends.

Alagar Kovil

Alagar god's temple — scenic, cool, river-side rituals.

Best time: early morning. Festival: Chithirai (April–May). Local prasadam: Jigarthanda, Malli dosa.
