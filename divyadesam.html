Featured Divine Shrines

Srirangam — Sri Ranganathaswamy

Tiruchirapalli, Tamil Nadu

Deity: Ranganatha (reclining Vishnu)

Sthala Varalaru: Cosmic island temple; praised as Bhuloka Vaikuntam.

Pariharam: Moksha blessings, relief from family obstacles.

Special: Vaikunta Ekadasi Paramapada Vasal.

Tirupati — Srinivasa

Tirumala, Andhra Pradesh

Deity: Venkateswara

Sthala Varalaru: Perumal manifested for Kali-yuga uplift.

Pariharam: Career, debts relief, dhana-labham (prosperity).

Special: Hair offering, Kalyanotsavam.

Oppiliappan — Thirunageswaram

Kumbakonam, Tamil Nadu

Deity: Uppiliappan (Salgrama form)

Sthala Varalaru: Perumal vowed never to taste salt.

Pariharam: Marriage delays, familial peace.

Special: Salt-free prasadam.
