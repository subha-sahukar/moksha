Vishnu — Avatars, Leelas & Temple Links

Vishnu in brief

Vishnu maintains cosmic order and descends as avatars to restore dharma. Temples dedicated to Vishnu (Divya Desams) are central to Vaishnava practice.

Representative stories

From Matsya (fish) to Kalki (future avatar), each incarnation addresses a cosmic need and is celebrated in temple iconography.

Rama’s life and the Ramaraya tradition—Rameswaram & Ramanathaswamy temple associations with Rama’s penance.

Krishna’s childhood stories, Gopis, and the Bhagavad Gita form central devotional material for many Vishnu temples.

Vishnu temples & festivals

Vaikunta Ekadashi, Brahmotsavam, and Utsavams form the major temple festivals. Divya Desams have deep Alvar hymn traditions associating each temple to a sung stanza.
