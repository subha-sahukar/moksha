Arupadai Veedu – The Six Sacred Abodes of Lord Murugan

  • Thiruparankundram: Marriage to Deivayanai; first abode.
  • Tiruchendur: Victory over Surapadman – the demon king.
  • Palani: Renunciation and wisdom; ascetic hills.
  • Swamimalai: Teaching “Om” to Shiva; heavenly knowledge.
  • Thiruthani: Homecoming and reunion; peace and devotion.
  • Pazhamudircholai: Fertile forest hills; Murugan’s divine bliss with both consorts Valli and Deivayanai.

Padal Petra Sthalam – 275 Sacred Shaivite Temples

Remarkable ancient temples glorified by the Tamil Saiva saint-poets (Nayanmars) through the Tevaram hymns, celebrated for their history, rituals, and the presence of special “Sthala Viruksham” trees and Tirtham tanks.

Divya Desams – The 108 Sacred Vishnu Temples

Vaishnava canonized sites, sung by the Alwars – each Divya Desam marked by a unique manifestation of Vishnu, magnificent festivals, and deep mythological significance.

Navagraha Sthalams – The Nine Planetary Temples

  • Dedicated to the Sun, Moon, Mars, Mercury, Jupiter, Venus, Saturn, Rahu, and Ketu.
  • Pilgrimage believed to bring harmony and astrological balance.
