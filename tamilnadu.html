Tamil Nadu — Stone & Sanctity

Explore the Chola heartland and beyond. Temples are grouped by district — each card a portal to history, architecture, and living ritual.

Kumbakonam District

Sarangapani Temple

Dedicated to Vishnu as Sarangapani. One of the 108 Divya Desams. The temple gopuram dominates the skyline with twelve tiers of carved mythology.

Airavatesvara Temple

A UNESCO heritage site built by Rajaraja Chola II. The stone chariot motif embodies motion and music in architecture.

Thanjavur District

Brihadeeswarar Temple

The Great Living Chola Temple, crowned with the world’s largest monolithic vimana capstone. Spiritual and structural awe in equal measure.

Madurai Region

Meenakshi Amman Temple

A twin sanctum for Meenakshi and Sundareswarar; city life revolves around its daily rituals and annual Chithirai festival.
