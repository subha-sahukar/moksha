Featured Divya Desams

Srirangam — Ranganathaswamy

Cosmic reclining Vishnu — Bhooloka Vaikuntam. Moksha blessings, Vaikunta Dwara.

Tirumala — Srinivasa

Kaliyuga deity; debt-relief, career growth, ancestral uplift.

Oppiliappan — Thirunageswaram

Saltless prasadam vow — sweet devotion, marriage boons.

“Where devotion flows, the Divine dwells.” — Vaishnava wisdom

The Twelve Azhwars

Poigai Azhwar
Mystical devotion; rain-water pond birth.
Bhoodath Azhwar
Fire-born; divine inner light.
Pey Azhwar
Seen Vishnu & Thayar together — ecstatic vision.
Thirumazhisai Azhwar
Uncompromising devotion; lived in surrender.
Nammalvar
“Vedam Tamil Seidha” poet — heart-melting hymns.
Madhurakavi Azhwar
Guru devotion — Nammalvar centric.
Kulashekara Azhwar
King-devotee; yearning for servant-life.
Periyalvar
Praise of Baby Krishna — protective psalms.
Andal
Only woman Azhwar — divine bridal mysticism (Thiruppavai).
Thondaradippodi
Garden-servant of Ranganatha.
Thirumangai Azhwar
Warrior-saint; fierce protector of temples.
Tōṉdaradippodi
Flower devotee offering garlands.

Divya Desam Yatra Guide

• Begin before sunrise, chant Govinda
• Avoid onion/garlic on darshan day
• Offer Tulasi, walk pradakshina calmly
• Visit Vaishnava Mutts when possible
• Read one Pasuram daily (Thiruppavai in Margazhi)

Om Namo Narayanaya ✨ May the Lord guide your steps.
