Narada — The Divine Sage & Mover of Stories

Narada in a nutshell

Narada is the celestial sage and devotee, known for his travels, devotion (bhakti) and role in catalyzing events through songs and counsel. His tales cross many Puranic events and are a rich source of devotional lore.

Why Narada matters for pilgrimages

Narada’s songs (bhajans) and the archetype of a wandering devotee resonate with pilgrimage culture. His stories can be used to create devotional audio/multimedia for your site.
