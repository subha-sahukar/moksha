Padal Petra Sthalam — Canonical Shaiva Shrines

What are Padal Petra Sthalams?

These are the 275 Shiva temples sung in the Tevaram hymns by the Tamil Nayanmar saints—Appar, Sambandar, and Sundarar. Each temple has unique sthala purana (local legend), a sacred tree (Sthala Viruksham), and a tirtha (sacred waterbody).

How to use this resource

This page groups Padal Petra Sthalams by district/region for practical pilgrimage planning. Each temple entry should ideally include: Sthala Purana, saint reference (which hymn), timings, and nearby transport.

Pilgrimage tips

  1. Plan visits by district to minimize transit time — many Padal Petra Sthalams cluster around Chola and Pandya heartlands.
  2. Check local festival calendars (Tamil months) for special pujas and pratishthas.
  3. Carry temple-appropriate attire and observe each shrine’s protocols.
